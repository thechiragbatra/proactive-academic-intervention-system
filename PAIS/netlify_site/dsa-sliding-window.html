📅 Sliding Window Attendance Detection

Rather than comparing total semester attendance, PAIS uses a 7-day sliding window to detect sudden "vanishing" patterns. A student maintaining 80% overall can still trigger an early warning if their last 7 days show 0–2 attendances — catching the problem weeks before the total percentage drops below 75%.
